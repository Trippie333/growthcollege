Frequently Asked Questions

Get clear answers to the most common questions about GrowthCollege.

Programs & Learning

What are the prerequisites for your programs?

Most of our core programs are beginner-friendly! A strong willingness to learn, basic computer literacy, and dedication are the main requirements. For advanced tracks like Smart Contract Development, prior programming experience is recommended.

How many hours per week should I commit?

Our programs are intensive and immersive. To succeed, you should plan to dedicate 20-25 hours per week to live sessions, project work, and independent study.

Do you provide career support?

Absolutely. Career readiness is built into our model. You'll get dedicated coaching on resumes, LinkedIn profiles, technical interview practice, and access to our network of hiring partners. Our goal is to support you until you land a role.

Payment & Financing

How does the Builder Plan work?

The Builder Plan is a straightforward installment plan. You secure your seat with a ₦110K deposit, followed by 6 monthly payments of ₦110K, for a fixed total cost of ₦660K. Payments start on your first day of class.

Do you offer scholarships or other payment options?

We periodically offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional applicants. We also have partnerships with vetted lending institutions. For more information on flexible options, please speak directly with our admissions advisors.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a risk-free initial period. If you withdraw within the first two weeks of your program, you will receive a full refund of your deposit and any installments paid.

Outcomes & Details

What kind of jobs do graduates get?

Graduates land roles like Full-Stack Developer, Data Analyst, UI/UX Designer, Junior Blockchain Developer, and Trading Operations Analyst, among others. Your specific outcome depends on your chosen program, effort, and the market conditions at the time of graduation.

Is the trading & crypto coursework financial advice?

No. Our Forex and Crypto programs are designed to provide educational knowledge and analytical skills. They do not constitute financial advice, a guarantee of profits, or a promise of specific investment outcomes. Trading involves significant risk of loss.

Do I need a specific computer or software?

You will need a reliable laptop (Mac, Windows, or Linux are all fine) and a stable internet connection. Specific software requirements (e.g., coding editors, Figma, trading simulators) will be provided before your program starts; much of it is free.
